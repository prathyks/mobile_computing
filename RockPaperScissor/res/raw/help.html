Help

Rules

  • Play game and choose either Rock, Paper or Scissor
  • You win or lose based on the opponent's choice
  • Rock beats scissor
  • Scissor beats paper
  • Paper beats rock

Instruction

  • You can choose your option by swiping fingers in any direction.
  • Swipe with 1 finger to choose Rock
  • Swipe with 2 finger to choose Paper
  • Swipe with 3 finger to choose Scissors
  • Swiping with more 3 fingers is not allowed
  • Swiping too slow or for a short length is also discarded
